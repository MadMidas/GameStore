Instructions

Upon entry to the "Shopping Cart" form, your selected items will be displayed along with their value. The "Remove" button will remove any item you wish from your cart, simply click on the item and click the remove button. The "Clear Cart" button will clear your cart of all items. The "Return to Shopping" button will return you to the "Shop Now" form. The "Check Out" button will generate a reciept for you and also generate the total for this transaction.

Interactives

Click the "Spire" logo to return to the "Shop Now" page. Click the "Shop Now" label to return to the "Shop Now" page.
