Menu Strip Items

The menu strip item "Shop Video Games" will direct you to the "Shop Now" form where you can shop for video games. The menu strip item "Help" will open this help file. The menu strip item "About" will open the "About" form where you will be informed about the programmer and use of this program. The menu strip item "Exit" will simply exit the progam.

Interactives

You can click on the "SHOP NOW" label that is in red and it will direct you to the "Shop Now" form.
