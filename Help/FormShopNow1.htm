Selecting games

The Data Grid Viewer will show the available games and their information. Upon finding a game that is desirable simply click on the game and choose the desired quantity of said game then click the "Add to Cart" button. This will add the selected amount of the game to your cart. To view your cart simply click the "Shopping cart" button.

Interactives

There are three ways to leave this form. The first is to click the "Shopping cart" button as previusly mentioned. The second is to click the "Spire" logo which will bring you to the home page. The third is to click the "Home" label in the top left corner which will bring you to the home page.
